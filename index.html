Rcipe

Measure Coffee

Measure 1-2 tablespoons of coffee grounds per cup of water.

Heat Water

Heat water to around 200°F (93°C) in a kettle or on the stovetop.

Combine Coffee & Water

Pour the heated water over the coffee grounds in a coffee maker or French press.

Steep Coffee

Allow the coffee to steep for 3-5 minutes, depending on the desired strength.

Serve & Enjoy

Strain the coffee into a cup and add desired flavorings, such as sugar or creamer.
